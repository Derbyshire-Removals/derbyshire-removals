About Derbyshire Removals

Derbyshire Removals has been providing professional moving services across the Midlands since 1988. With over 35 years of experience, we've built a reputation for reliability, care, and exceptional customer service.

Our team consists of fully trained professionals who understand that moving can be stressful. That's why we go the extra mile to ensure your belongings are handled with the utmost care and your move goes as smoothly as possible.

As a family-owned business, we take pride in our personalized approach. We're not just moving furniture; we're helping families and businesses start new chapters in their lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide stress-free moving experiences through professional service, transparent pricing, and a genuine care for our customers' needs. We aim to be the most trusted removal company in Derbyshire and beyond.

Our Values

Reliability

We show up on time, every time, and deliver on our promises. You can count on us to be there when you need us.

Professionalism

Our team is trained to handle your belongings with care and respect, ensuring everything arrives safely at your new location.

Customer Focus

We listen to your needs and tailor our services accordingly, making your satisfaction our top priority.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced movers who are passionate about providing excellent service. Each member undergoes thorough training in proper handling techniques, packing methods, and customer service to ensure your move is handled professionally from start to finish.
